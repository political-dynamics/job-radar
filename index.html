03 Transparent by design

What the score
actually means.

The match score combines role alignment (30%), CV skill overlap (40%), seniority (15%), Hamburg/remote fit (10%), and domain relevance (5%). It is a prioritization aid, not a hiring probability.

How salary estimates work

Employer-published ranges pass through unchanged. Otherwise the model starts from Bundesagentur für Arbeit medians, applies a role-family and level factor, then a small profile-fit adjustment. Estimated ranges are intentionally broad and shown with medium or low confidence.

Where jobs come from

The updater visits permitted official career pages, refreshes Experimentation Jobs and the community-documented BA Jobsuche endpoint once daily, and uses at most ten Brave Search requests per UTC day. Dated results remain for 21 days. Jobs with unclear, on-site, or region-restricted work models stay visible but are marked and ranked lower; weapons, defence-specific, gambling, tobacco, and adult-industry roles are excluded. Google Search, LinkedIn, and Bundesagentur web pages are not scraped.
